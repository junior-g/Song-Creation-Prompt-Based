## Melody Pass (C# minor — Aeolian)

**Scale reference:** C# (la) · D# (ti) · E (do) · F# (re) · G# (mi) · A (fa) · B (sol)
Notation = note letter (solfège) with approximate rhythm. Verses sit in the low-mid range (rap-sung, A2–E4); choruses lift (up to G4) for the anthem.

### Verse 1 (low-mid, rhythmic/spoken-sung over | C#m | A | E | B |)
- "पंद्रह की उम्र से मैं कर रहा मेहनत" → C# C# D# E (la la ti do), mostly 8th notes, land on E (do) — half
- "god और father की बस रही रहमत" → E E D# C# B (do do ti la sol), 8ths, resolve down to B (sol)
- "पर ना कोई Godfather जो रास्ता दिखाएं" → C# E F# E D# (la do re do ti), rising then easing
- "गिर-गिर कर मैंने खुद रास्ते बनाएं" → E D# C# B C# (do ti la sol la), settle on tonic C# (la)

### Chorus (lifted, sustained, legato over | A | E | B | C#m |)
- "No godfather, I am walking alone" → F# G# A G# F# E (re mi fa mi re do); peak A (fa) on "walk-", descend on "alone" → C# (la)
- " na rukunga, na jhukunga — will walk very long" → E F# G# F# E D# C# (do re mi re do ti la); climb on "jhukunga", resolve long on C# (la) — held

### Verse 2 (same contour family as Verse 1, slight variation over | C#m | A | E | B |)
- "सिर्फ एक रास्ते का मैं वो आदमी नहीं" → C# D# E E D# C# (la ti do do ti la)
- "और रुकता किसी के मैं वास्ते नहीं" → C# C# D# E D# B (la la ti do ti sol)
- "हर तीसरे साल कुछ नया है किया" → E F# E D# C# (do re do ti la), gentle arc
- "और क्यों लिख रहा — शायद तुम्हे दिख गया" → D# C# B C# (ti la sol la), land on tonic

### Bridge (building, wider intervals over | F#m | C#m | A | B |)
- "मंजिल ही मिले ये जरूरी तो नहीं" → F# F# E D# E (re re do ti do), restrained start
- "और मंजिल पर रुक जाएं हम वो भी नहीं" → F# G# A G# F# E (re mi fa mi re do), rising tension
- "मंजिलों के लिए पहाड़ काट जाएं" → G# A B A G# (mi fa sol fa mi), climbing higher
- "दशरथ मांझी की तरह रास्ता बनाएं" → A B C#5 B A G# F# (fa sol la↑ sol fa mi re) — peak on high C#5 (la, octave up), the emotional summit

### Final Chorus (same as chorus, top harmony stack adds 3rd above; lead may push climactic line up an octave)
- Hook melody identical to Chorus; on "and will walk very long" lead can ascend to E5 (do, octave up) for the triumphant lift, gang vocals on "walking alone".

### Outro (exposed, slow, resolving over | C#m | A | B | C#m |)
- "Will walk till the last breath" → E D# C# B (do ti la sol), slow, breathy
- "and achieve all the dreams before death" → C# D# E D# C# (la ti do ti la), final long C# (la) — fade
